"Linking Multiple RSS Feeds"

Published: Mon 28 May 2012
By Dustin Farris

In blog.

tags: RSS

I split my RSS feed the other day to filter technical blog posts from the rest by creating a /feeds/ page that offers the option. Little did I know that these options can be presented right in the head of an HTML document just by adding additional "links"

{% raw %}

    <link
        rel="alternate"
        type="application/rss+xml"
        title="RSS 2.0 - All blog entries"
        href="{% url blog:feed-all %}">
    <link
        rel="alternate"
        type="application/rss+xml"
        title="RSS 2.0 - Technical entries only"
        href="{% url blog:feed-techie %}">

{% endraw %}

Done.
