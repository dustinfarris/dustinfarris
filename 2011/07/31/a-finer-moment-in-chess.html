"A finer moment in chess"

chess

I love chess, but that should not be confused with me actually being any good at it. Every now and then, though, I surprise myself. In this Sunday morning game, I sacrifice both of my bishops to open up black's king after he castled. After attempting to pin my queen, I move in for mate with Rh5#.
