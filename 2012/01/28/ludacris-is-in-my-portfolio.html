"Ludacris is in My Portfolio!"

Published: Sat 28 January 2012
By Dustin Farris

In blog.

tags:

Earlier this month my Wonder colleagues and I wrapped up what is probably my biggest project to date: a website for the new line of SOUL by Ludacris headphones. And yes, I think that's pretty cool.

Building the website was a lot of fun, and allowed me to explore Django even further, as well as pick up a few project management techniques. Ludacris even came into our office for a photo shoot!

The true end result was motivation to finally update my portfolio.
